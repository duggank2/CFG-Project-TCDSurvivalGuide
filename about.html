About

Welcome to Trinity! It can take a while to get used to things and make friends but we're here to share our tips and tricks to making the most of your time here.

Trinity College, officially the College of the Holy and Undivided Trinity of Queen Elizabeth near Dublin, is the sole constituent college of the University of Dublin, a research university located in Dublin, Ireland.

Located on College Green, Dublin 2, it is ranked as Ireland's #1 Univeristy. It has a historic campus, at the core of which you can find The Book of Kells.

Trying to balance academic work with social life may be quite hard. This website is designed to help you do just that. You can find here the best places on campus to get free food while also participating in society events and also the best student discounts.

Hope you enjoy your time here!
